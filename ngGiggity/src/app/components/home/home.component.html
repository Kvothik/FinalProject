About Gig-City:

Gig-City is online marketplace that matches freelance labor with local and remote demand, allowing consumers to find help with everyday tasks


Registered users may list and bid on one-off jobs, or Gigs, online. Both Buyer and Seller can feel safe using Gig-City because we allow each to rate the other, which generates a user-reputation.


Gigs listed are diverse - ranging from inexpensive & simple, one-off jobs, to larger & more involved tasks.
